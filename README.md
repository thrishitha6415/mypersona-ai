# 🚀 PersonaAI – AI-Powered Digital Identity System

> **Your Identity. Your Journey. Your Future.**

PersonaAI is an AI-powered Digital Identity System that transforms scattered academic and professional documents into an intelligent, searchable digital identity. It helps students organize, analyze, and showcase their achievements while providing AI-driven career insights and recommendations.

## 🌐 Live Demo

🔗https://mypersona-ai.lovable.app/auth

## 📌 Problem Statement

Students often store resumes, certificates, internship letters, project reports, and achievements across multiple folders, emails, and cloud drives. Finding the right document when needed becomes difficult.

PersonaAI solves this by using Artificial Intelligence to understand, organize, connect, and retrieve user information intelligently.

# 🚀 Workflow

1. Register/Login
2. Complete your profile
3. Upload resumes or certificates
4. AI analyzes uploaded documents
5. Skills and achievements are extracted
6. PersonaAI generates your digital persona
7. AI creates career recommendations
8. Search your information using natural language
